I am a PhD candidate in the laboratory of Jeff Magee at Baylor College of Medicine and HHMI. My current research aims to improve our understanding of the mechanistic basis of neurobiological learning and adaptive behavior. Most models of neurobiological learning are based on synaptic plasticity. Therefore, I am investigating the forms and functions of synaptic plasticity during adaptive behaviors.
